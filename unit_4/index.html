Niteo Soap Co.

Niteo Soap Co. was created out of inspired boredom. We make small-batch cold-process soap made from ecologically conscious ingredients. Each soap is unique and made with care. We never use Phalates or Unsustainable Palm Oil. Our products are never tested on animals.
Here at Niteo Soap, we believe in:

  • Quality ingredients and products
  • Finding happiness in the little things
  • Supporting local business and community
  • Our customers are our neighbors
